Saudi Arabia

About Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is a land of cultural significance, breathtaking landscapes, and rapid modernization. The country is the birthplace of Islam and home to Mecca and Medina, the two holiest cities for Muslims. Its diverse landscapes range from the vast Rub' al Khali (Empty Quarter) desert to the Red Sea's coral reefs. The ancient Nabatean tombs of Al-Ula and the futuristic NEOM city project showcase its blend of past and future. Visitors can enjoy authentic Saudi experiences like traditional Bedouin hospitality, camel rides, and aromatic Arabic coffee served with dates. The Kingdom is also investing heavily in tourism, making it an emerging global destination.

Top Attractions

  • Mecca: The spiritual center of Islam, with the sacred Kaaba at its heart.
  • Medina: Known for the Prophet’s Mosque, an important pilgrimage site.
  • Al-Ula: Home to stunning rock formations and the ancient site of Mada’in Saleh.
  • Edge of the World: A dramatic desert cliff offering breathtaking views.
  • Red Sea Diving: Pristine underwater ecosystems ideal for divers.
  • Riyadh: A modern capital with landmarks like the Kingdom Centre Tower.
