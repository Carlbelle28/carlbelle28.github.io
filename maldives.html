Maldives

About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise of overwater villas, turquoise lagoons, and pristine white-sand beaches. Known for its luxury resorts and world-class diving spots, this island nation is ideal for relaxation and adventure. Its coral reefs, teeming with marine life, attract snorkelers and divers from around the world. Local culture is reflected in the Maldivian cuisine, with dishes like mas huni and fresh seafood. The Maldives also offers traditional music and dance performances such as boduberu.

Top Attractions

  • Malé: The bustling capital with mosques and local markets.
  • Baa Atoll: A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve with vibrant marine life.
  • Vaadhoo Island: Known for its bioluminescent glowing beaches.
  • Hulhumalé Beach: A tranquil escape near Malé.
  • Ari Atoll: A top destination for diving and underwater adventures.
