China

About China

China, a vast and diverse nation, offers a journey through millennia of history and modern innovation. From ancient wonders like the Great Wall and Forbidden City to the futuristic skyline of Shanghai, China is a country of contrasts. Its rich cultural heritage is evident in traditional festivals like Chinese New Year and Mid-Autumn Festival. Chinese cuisine, ranging from Peking duck to dim sum, is celebrated worldwide. The country's natural wonders, such as Zhangjiajie’s Avatar Mountains, are equally awe-inspiring.

Top Attractions

  • Great Wall of China: A monumental feat of ancient engineering.
  • Forbidden City: A sprawling palace complex in Beijing.
  • Terracotta Army: Thousands of life-sized statues guarding an emperor’s tomb.
  • Yangtze River: The longest river in Asia, with stunning river cruises.
  • Jiuzhaigou Valley: Known for its multicolored lakes and waterfalls.
