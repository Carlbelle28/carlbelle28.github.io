About Canada

Canada, the second-largest country in the world, is celebrated for its breathtaking landscapes, multicultural cities, and warm-hearted people. Visitors can explore the Rocky Mountains, marvel at Niagara Falls, and experience the Northern Lights in the Yukon. Urban hubs like Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal offer vibrant arts scenes, diverse cuisine, and rich histories. Known for its maple syrup and poutine, Canada also hosts lively festivals such as the Calgary Stampede and the Montreal Jazz Festival.
